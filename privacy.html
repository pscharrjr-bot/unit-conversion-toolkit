Privacy Policy

This page explains the basic privacy approach for Unit Conversion Toolkit.

General use

Unit Conversion Toolkit is designed to work as a simple public utility. You do not need to create an account or submit personal information to use the converters.

Most conversions are intended to happen directly in your browser using the values you enter on the page.

Information that may be collected

Like most websites, basic technical data may be collected automatically by hosting providers, analytics tools, or similar services. This can include information such as browser type, device type, approximate location based on IP address, referring pages, and general page usage data.

Contact messages

If you send an email, the information you provide in that message may be used to respond to your question, investigate a bug, or review a suggestion for improving the site.

Future changes

If analytics, advertising, affiliate features, or additional site functionality are added later, this policy may be updated to reflect those changes.

Policy updates

Privacy details may be revised from time to time as the site evolves. Any meaningful updates should be reflected on this page.
