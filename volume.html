How this volume converter works

This tool converts the amount you enter into a shared milliliter base, then converts that value into the target unit you select. That makes it useful for quick kitchen math, drink measurements, recipe scaling, and everyday liquid conversions.

Supported units: mL, L, fl oz, cups, pints, quarts, gallons, teaspoons, and tablespoons.

Example

If you want to convert 2 liters to fluid ounces, the result is about 67.63 fl oz.

That is helpful when you are comparing bottle sizes, converting recipe quantities, or checking serving amounts in different measurement systems.

Common uses

  • Converting recipe ingredients between teaspoons, tablespoons, cups, and liters
  • Checking beverage sizes in milliliters, ounces, or gallons
  • Scaling soups, sauces, and baking mixes up or down
  • Comparing product packaging across metric and US customary units
